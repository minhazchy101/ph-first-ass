Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation in Bangladesh 2.0 drives technology, entrepreneurship, education, collaboration, and sustainability

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh 2.0 prioritizes resilience, green energy, agriculture, and biodiversity.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure in Bangladesh 2.0 emphasizes connectivity, modernization, resilience, efficiency, and sustainability.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh 2.0 fosters education, equality, opportunities, inclusion, and innovation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The New Bangladesh envisions a future driven by transformative ideas, fostering innovation across sectors to create sustainable growth and global competitiveness. With a clear vision of inclusivity and technological advancement, it embraces digitalization and green practices. However, challenges such as economic disparities, climate vulnerability, and resource management require bold strategies, collaboration, and resilience. By addressing these hurdles, New Bangladesh can achieve its aspiration of becoming a dynamic and empowered nation.
